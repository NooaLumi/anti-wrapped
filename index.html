? What is ekphrasis?

Ekphrasis is a short, vivid description that puts your feelings about the album or song into words. Below are some examples on how to write one.

Mimetic/Phonetic

Sentences can convey rhythm and words can mimic sound.

Examples

your. limbs. are. now. forefeit. to. the. rhythm.

bass-biased bullfrog throat opera

soothing timbre~ / funk attack!

Lexical Pairing

Pick two striking words that clash or harmonize.

Examples

chaos woven / stars dimmed

fruit bouquet / pineapple pizza

Sensory

Combine a sensory experience with a concrete image.

Examples

scent of wet bark in a forest after days of rain.

bitter lemon yuck but always thirsty for more.

Texture

Combine texture and mood words.

Examples

pain wrapped in cotton...

crumbly dough sweetness~

Scene

Describe a scene or experience.

Examples

sunkissed leaves under a streetlight where a large tree once stood.

a glass of milk with your camel after crossing the vast sandbox.

Simile

Pick an abstract simile. The less it has to do with the subject, the better.

Examples

vocals like a tree split in twain by thunder.

guitars like tigers doing priouettes.
